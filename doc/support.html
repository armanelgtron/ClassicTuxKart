MAILING LISTS

There are three TuxKart mailing lists. In order to keep the spam off the lists, it's set up so you have to subscribe in order to send mail. To do that, visit:
  • http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/tuxkart-devel
    A list for people who wish to contribute to the development of TuxKart - this list will have a high technical content and we'll try to keep discussions there focussed and on-topic.

  • http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/tuxkart-users
    A list for TuxKart users - this would be the correct place to ask questions, report bugs and generally hang out with fellow players.

  • http://lists.sourceforge.net/mailman/listinfo/tuxkart-announce
    A very low traffic, heavily moderated list where I'll announce new versions of TuxKart.

Please do not send me personal mail in situations when one of the public mailing lists would be an appropriate forum - I hate it when people do that.
